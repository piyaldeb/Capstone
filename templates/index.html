Sonar data is data collected from sonar systems, which are used to detect and locate underwater objects using sound waves. This data typically consists of acoustic signals that are transmitted into the water and the resulting echoes reflected off objects in the water. Sonar data can be represented in various formats, including time-domain signals, frequency-domain signals, or processed feature vectors derived from the raw acoustic signals.
